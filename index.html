Alchemy Workshop

The Alchemy Workshop is an interactive songwriting and performance event that is memorable, fun and very engaging. What we are really working on is exercising your collective team’s creativity and creating the opportunity to push beyond each individual’s comfort zone. Participants who begin the session with the belief that they aren't very creative or know nothing about songwriting come away amazed at what they contributed and created. The tangible deliverable is creating something of clear value (the song) in a surprisingly short time frame. The lessons applied to your business are big, lasting and impactful.
