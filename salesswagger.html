Finding your Sales Swagger

Finding your Sales Swagger is a unique, engaging, and entertaining session filled with energy, surprises and significant participation. It uses Rock Stars and Rock Star stories as the context to reveal the power of confidence and the use of “performance” to enhance your effectiveness in sales.

Becoming a standout Star in professional selling requires competence, confidence and commitment. When it comes to confidence, just the right amount will contribute to you being a Star. However, if your confidence falls short, your performance will fall short. On the other hand, too much confidence in a given situation, won’t end well for you or your reputation.

This well researched, highly customized session will offer the awareness, insights and skills to help sales professionals find and use just the right amount of confidence in any sales situation. We often refer to this type of confidence as “sales swagger”. Through fascinating research, real stories, video clips and valuable exercises we will deliver a memorable session for your sales team.

The presenters:

Tommy McWilliams, Emmy® Winning, Grammy® Nominated musician with 25+ Years in the music business including: Playing in front of 80,000 plus audiences, major network television appearances, awarded over twenty five gold and platinum certified records and the unique experience of coaching and developing young Rock Stars.

Gregg Baron, Certified Management Consultant, Researcher, Author and Global Sales Trainer. Some of his clients include: Universal Orlando Resort, Honeywell, Royal Caribbean Cruise Lines, AAA, IBM Europe, Celebrity Cruises, American Express Travel, Club Med, MSC Cruises, and Cigna.

This session is perfect for Global, National or Regional sales meetings. It can be done as a 1 – 2 hour highlight or designed with more skills for a half-day session. Audiences between 60 and 200 are ideal. This session is always customized for each sales culture, brand identity and appropriate participation of the audience.

Every engagement is designed for the unique priorities of the audience.
