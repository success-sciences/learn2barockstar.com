Alchemy Workshop

Ultimately your business is about people coming together to create, enhance or reposition value. In The Alchemy Workshop we utilize the process of creating music as a metaphor for helping your team have breakthroughs in collaboration, innovation, creativity and cooperation, all of which supports value creation.

The Alchemy Workshop is an interactive songwriting and performance event that is fun and engaging. What we are really working on is exercising your collective team’s creativity and creating the opportunity to push beyond each individual’s comfort zone in a fun, safe, environment.

Leaders, sales teams, IT people, financial managers anyone in your workforce can have a fresh and exciting new way to connect with each other, collaborate and perform like a Rock Star.

We will break your team into groups. Write a song on the spot and prepare to perform is.

When the teams get to perform the songs they have created in front of the other groups, it's like nothing else you've ever done. They get to receive the over-whelming support from an audience of their peers. Everyone has to be on stage eventually, so everyone is in the same situation creating an environment of creativity and collaboration.

Every engagement is designed for the unique priorities of the audience.
